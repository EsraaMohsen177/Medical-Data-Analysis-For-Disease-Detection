About Our Project

Empowering health through awareness

Our goal is to create an intuitive, secure, and informative platform that helps users assess their risk for cancer, heart disease, and diabetes through a personalized questionnaire. By providing accurate risk predictions, actionable recommendations, and educational resources, we aim to empower users to take control of their health and seek timely medical advice.

This academic project was developed by students at Ain Shams University as part of their graduation requirements under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Azza Abd-El Rahman.
